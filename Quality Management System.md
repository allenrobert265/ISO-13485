**DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS**
**1.quality manual**
it is the rule book of the company
it must include the scope,quality policy,departments,procedures and intraction between processes
**2.medical device file**
every device manufactured by the company must have its own file
it must include the intented use,specs,design,manufacturing process,risk management,IFU,regulatory requirements
**3.Document Control**
here documents means it tells ppl what to do. set of intrustions. like SOPs.
documents are controled by reviewing and approving
if the document is resived, the changes must be updated and it must be used by everyone
**4.record control**
records are evidence that the work is done or the procedure is followed
