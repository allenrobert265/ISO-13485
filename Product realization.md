**Control of Monitoring and Measuring Equipment**


Control of Monitoring and Measuring Equipment means ensuring that all equipment used to inspect, test, monitor, or measure medical devices is accurate, calibrated, maintained, and suitable for its intended purpose.

S – Suitable equipment
C – Calibrated
I – Identified
V – Verified
P – Protected
M – Maintained
R – Records maintained
